Welcome!

I'm David Grady

Environmental scientist | Data analyst | Science communicator

I'm PhD graduate and an environmental earth scientist from Aberystwyth University. I love exploring different environments, collecting and working with interesting data and discovering new things about our world! My passion is engaging with people and problems. I love the challenge of finding out how something can be done and working on a solution. You can often find me engrossed in analysing data and keeping up with the latest issues, policy and methods, or even developing new skills like HTML coding to create web pages like this one. The main aspects of my career have been:

1) Analysing natural and human environments to understand current and past environmental change;

2) Quantitative analyses of environments over long time-frames, with a particular focus on hydrological and ecological change;

3) Communicating and advocating earth and data science through teaching and volunteering positions.

It is incredibly satisfying to be working hard on a complex problem and figure out a great solution whether that is to determine environmental change or impacts, a coding issue, the best way to analyse a database or how to get complicated science through to schoolkids. I am determined to apply and continue developing my skillset by working on challenging projects with an amazing team (the bigger and more multi-disciplinary the better!). If you’re interested in making this happen, let’s get in touch. Please take some time to explore the rest of my website and have a look at my CV.
